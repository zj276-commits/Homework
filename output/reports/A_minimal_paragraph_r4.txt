Leaders should be aware that Exercise Riverdale is a full‑scale county emergency drill taking place from 06:00 to 14:00 local time on the scheduled exercise day, with unified staging at Depot 7 (grid RC‑17) in River County. The Emergency Operations Center will operate at Level 2 partial, and liaison officers from Fire, EMS, and Public Works have been assigned. At 09:30 a simulated chlorine odor will be reported near the old mill, but this is purely role‑play with no real hazard or injuries; all reported casualties are actors. Communications will use the 800 MHz county talkgroup TAC‑2 as the primary channel, with satellite phones on standby for branch directors as backup. A single public‑information‑officer statement will be released at 11:00, clarifying that the exercise is underway and there is no danger to the public. After the event, a formal after‑action report is due ten business days later to the county EM office, and note that potable water bladders are not part of this scenario’s logistics.
