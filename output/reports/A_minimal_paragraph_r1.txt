During Exercise Riverdale, scheduled for 06:00–14:00 local time on the exercise day, the unified staging area is located at Depot 7 (grid RC‑17) in River County. The EOC is operating at Level 2 partial with liaison officers from Fire, EMS, and Public Works. At 09:30 a simulated chlorine odor will be introduced near the old mill as an inject, but all hazards are role‑played and there are no real injuries; any reported casualties are part of the drill plan. Communications will be conducted on the primary 800 MHz talkgroup TAC‑2, with satellite phones reserved for branch directors as backup. A single public information statement is set for 11:00 announcing that an exercise is in progress and that there is no danger to the public. After‑action reporting requirements call for a formal AAR to be submitted N+10 business days to the county EM office, and logistics notes indicate that potable‑water bladders are not deployed in this version of the scenario.
