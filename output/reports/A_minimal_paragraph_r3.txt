Leaders should note that Exercise Riverdale, a full‑scale county emergency drill scheduled from 06:00 to 14:00 local time on the exercise day, will convene at River County’s unified staging area, Depot 7 (grid RC‑17). The EOC will operate at Level 2 partial, with liaison officers from Fire, EMS and Public Works committed throughout the window. At 09:30 a simulated chlorine odor will be reported near the old mill; although the scenario is purely fictional, response protocols must assume a hazardous‑materials release and incorporate the role‑players posing as casualties, none of whom represent real injuries. Primary communications will run on the 800 MHz county talkgroup TAC‑2, with satellite phones assigned to branch directors as backup. A single public‑information‑officer statement is set for 11:00 to convey that the exercise is underway and poses no danger to the public. An after‑action report is required within ten business days of completion and submission to the county EM office. Note that potable water bladders are not part of this scenario’s logistics plan.
