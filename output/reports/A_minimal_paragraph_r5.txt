The current Riverdale drill is a full‑scale county emergency exercise scheduled for 06:00–14:00 local time, taking place at the unified staging area of Depot 7 (grid RC‑17) in River County. The EOC is operating at Level 2 partial, with liaison officers from Fire, EMS and Public Works committed to the response. At 09:30 a simulated chlorine odor will be reported near the old mill; this inject is purely for training and poses no real hazard. No actual injuries are expected — all casualties are role‑players — and the exercise will conclude with a scheduled public‑information statement at 11:00 confirming that the activity is an exercise and that there is no danger to the public. Primary communications will run on the 800 MHz county talkgroup TAC‑2, with satellite phones on standby for branch directors, and logistical support will not include potable water bladders in this version. After‑action reporting is required within ten business days of the exercise.
